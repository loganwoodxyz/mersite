Our Philosophy

Because childhood isn't something to rush through. It's something to treasure.

My goal is to create a warm, home-away-from-home where children feel safe, loved, and excited to learn each day — and where families feel confident knowing their little one is known, valued, and genuinely cared for.

🎨

Play-based learning

Sensory activities, crafts, books, music, and movement — every day is hands-on, curiosity-driven, and full of exploration.

📋

Daily updates

A shared Google Doc with meals, naps, diapering, milestones, and photos — so you never miss a moment of their day.

🏡

Family-centered care

Every child who walks through our door is treated like family — encouraged to build confidence, independence, and happy memories.

About Marissa

Hi, I'm Marissa!

I'm a wife to Logan and a mom to our daughter, Mia — the inspiration behind this program and the reason I want every child in my care to experience a childhood filled with wonder, creativity, kindness, and happy memories.

I have over 15 years of experience working with children from infancy through elementary age, most recently as a Lead Infant Teacher. I hold a Bachelor's Degree from the University of Iowa in Sociology with a minor in American Sign Language. I love photography, crafts, and capturing the little moments that make childhood so special — and I bring that creativity into our days together.
